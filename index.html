About Me

After graduating from Oberlin College in 2016 with a degree in Economics, I went to work for the Aspen Skiing Company in Aspen, Colorado. While there, I learned about my passion for learning, self-development and the outdoors.

I left Aspen to pursue my career in Real Estate, which at the time was my goal. While working at Colliers International in San Francisco, California I had the great opportunity to discover myself. While solo migrating a SQL database with over 70,000+ points of information, I found something I love - coding.

To pursue this new found love, I attended App Academy and became a Software Engineer.

I am a software engineer who has worked on projects that use a variety of technologies, including Ruby Rails, React, JavaScript and Redux, and Django/Python.

I am passionate about learning, reading books (inclusive of self-help books), cooking, road biking, soccer, and skiing.
