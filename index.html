About Me

After graduating from Oberlin College in 2016 with a degree in economics, I went to Aspen, Colorado, to work for the Aspen Skiing Company. While there, I learned about my passion for learning and self-development.

I left Aspen to pursue my career in Real Estate, which at the time was my goal. While working at Colliers International in San Francisco, I had the great opportunity to discover myself. While solo migrating a SQL database with over 70,000+ points of information , I found something I love - coding.

To pursue this new found love, I attended App Academy and became a Software Engineer.

I am a software engineer who has worked on projects that use a variety of technologies, including Ruby Rails, React, and Redux, as well as knowledge in PHP and Python.

I am passionate about learning, reading books (inclusive of self help books), cooking, road biking, soccer, and skiing.
